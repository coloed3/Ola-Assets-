Luis & Michelle Felix

Chef & Event Coordinator

Luis Felix Bio

Felix was born in Christiansted, St. Croix United States Virgin Islands. Coming from a simple and humble home of fishermen, life revolved around ocean life and food. His culinary journey has taken him from U.S Virgin Islands, Puerto Rico all over the east coast, from Florida to Boston and then Oahu, finally where his wife and he call home, on the Island of Hawaii (Big Island).


Felix entered the culinary arena under the direction of many mentors throughout his epic Culinary Arts Journey. First with Master Chef Hiroshi Nagouchi at the Renaissance Orlando Resort at Sea World, where he held an American Culinary Federation apprenticeship thru his culinary education held at Le Cordon Blue in Orlando, Florida. Then mentored by Director of Food and Beverage at Hilton Daytona Beach Ocean Front Resort Stan Roper, Mr. Roper molded Felix in becoming a successful Executive Chef.


Felix traveled to Boston taking a culinary position at State Room where he assisted in the Barack Obama’s campaign event dinner. Felix then traveled to Island of Oahu to expand his knowledge in surfing and Hawaii Regional Cuisine, where he worked and learned from Alan Wong at Alan Wong’s Honolulu, his love and passion for the Hawaiian culture has made his Ohana call these beautiful Islands home. Felix would like the opportunity to share the light of the Aloha Spirit by educating and creating memorable sustainable cuisine at your event. Felix’s company Hawaii’s Ola Creations LLC specializes in creating culinary masterpieces, utilizing local organic produce and game with menus built by you the Host of honor with the guidance and knowledge of a passionate and skilled Culinary Mentor with lots of Aloha.


Michelle Felix Bio

Michelle was born in Durban, South Africa from Indian descend. She spend most of her childhood in Africa and eventually moved to England where she studied, work, traveled and work in various humanitarian projects. She came to the United States when she received a scholarship to study in California. She is currently a flight attendant for American Airlines while working on her MS in Psychology. She is a proud contributor to her husband’s unique, talented and extraordinary cuisine journey. We have both mastered and explored our skills in the culinary field by integrating our cultures (Indian, African & Caribbean) together and designing some of the most unique flavorful dishes ever made. Our philosophy in life is to live simple, be humble, give back to the community and preserve the beauty of Mother Nature (proudly a sustainable product corporation).

“We love to serve and we love what we do”
